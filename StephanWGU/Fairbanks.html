City of Fairbanks

    Fairbanks Facts

  1. By 1911, Fairbanks was Alaska territory’s largest city, with a population of 3,500.
  2. Gold is still commercially mined in two locations near Fairbanks.
  3. Fairbanks is located under the Auroral Oval.
  4. Nearly half of Fairbank’s population is made up of military personnel.
  5. Aviation and the military are two major industries in Fairbanks.

Wild Life

Lynx: These gorgeous creatures are found across most of mainland Alaska.

Fox: The red fox can sometimes be seen darting across the road in woody, brushy areas.

Caribou: Caribou are distributed in 32 herds in the Arctic tundra, mountain tundra and northern forests of Alaska.
